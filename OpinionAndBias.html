Opinions and Bias: Example One

The prime minister made a big decision yesterday and there are a lot of articles about it on the internet. You see one titled, “Prime Minister Makes Huge Mistake”, one titled “Prime Minister Makes a Bold Decision”, and another titled “The Arguments For and Against the Prime Minister’s Decision”. How should you figure out what’s going on?
